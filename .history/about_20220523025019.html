About Us

An energy servicing company that specializes in the supply and distribution of petroleum products to its customers. Also, procure, install, repairs, and maintains diesel generators of all capacities.

Objective

In support to the national grid and in provision of jobs, we are ensuring people are powered at the fastest and most reliable way.

Vision

To ensure quality, safety, and swift delivery of our products and services to our customers; and to ensure that the stakeholders are satisfied.

Mission

We champion quality, and on-time delivery of our products i.e., AGO and HHK, and services i.e., procurement, installation, and maintenance of diesel generators.
